About Me

I am a nanotechnologist and multidisciplinary scientist with a keen interest in exploring the intersections of chemical biology, materials chemistry, and molecular nanotechnology. My research focuses on understanding nanoscale interactions, where I bridge the fields of biology and chemistry to investigate how these tiny structures can influence larger biological systems. Through my work, I aim to uncover innovative solutions that leverage nanoscale phenomena for advancements in various scientific domains.
